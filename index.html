U V I C - N E U R O T E C H

Committed to inclusivity and innovation!

The UVic Neurotech Club provides an educational and welcoming environment for students of all backgrounds and majors to learn about and work with Neurotechnology. We utilize cutting edge hardware and methodology to create technology using and related to brain-computer interfaces. The work and research we do is very multidisciplinary, with a span of computer science, hardware engineering, and neuroscience/biology. Neurotechnology has applications to many disciplines and areas of life, and as a field strives to develop inventive and ingenuitive technology to positively impact our understanding of the brain and mind in addition to individual lives.
