Club Goals

Many big Canadian Universities have a huge Neurotech club of 25+ students of all different areas of study. NerurotechX is an organization that hosts a University Neurotech competition that encouraged students to create projects that can better the world. On of our main goals for Uvic Neurotech is to give our best shot at eventially winning the competion and creating something to be proud of.
